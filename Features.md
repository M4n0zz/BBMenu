## BBMenu Script Features

### **Run**

* Doubles Overworld speed.
* Holding **A** activates super-speed (similar to emulator fast-forward).

### **Slip**

* Holding **B** lets you walk through walls safely.
* Prevents crashes when reaching map borders (use with caution).

### **Repel**

* Enables a permanent Repel effect.

### **Beast**

* Maxes out your battle active Pokémon’s stats.
* Almost all moves become one-hit KOs.
* Non-damaging moves cause the opponent to flinch.

### **StealRun**

* Allows stealing an enemy trainer’s Pokémon.
* Allows running from a battle.
* In both scenarios the battle ends and the game counts it as a win.

### **Stealth**

Stops all map scripts causing the following effects:
* Avoids wild trainers, blocked paths, and forced interactions.
* Freezes map objects (can break events), so use carefully.

### **Fly**

* Allows using Fly anytime without needing a Pokémon for it.
* Allows travelling to any unlocked Fly destination.

### **Heal**

* Fully heals your party instantly.

### **PC**

* Opens the PC instantly.

### **Items**

Shows all obtainable items (plus some glitched ones).
* **A:** Choose quantity (1–256).
* **D-Pad + B:** Remove the last item in your bag.

### **Moves**

* Teach any move to any party Pokémon.

### **Pokemon**

* Pokédex-style list of all Pokémon.
* Add any Pokémon at any level to your party.

### **Wilds**

* Pokédex-style list of all Pokémon.
* Trigger a wild encounter with any Pokémon at any level.

### **Trainers**

* List of all trainers in the game.
* Battle any trainer instantly.

### **Instatext**

* All dialogue text appears instantly.

### **FillDex**

* Completes the Pokédex with all **seen** and **owned** entries.

### **Badges**

* Gives all gym badges.

### **Cash**

* Sets money to the maximum.

### **Coins**

* Sets Game Corner coins to the maximum.

### **Clone**

* Clones the Pokémon in **party slot 1** into **slot 2**, replacing whatever is there.

### **Pong**

Starts a Pong minigame.
* Game speed increases as your score rises.
* Shows current score and high score.
* High score is saved to the save file.

### **Snake**

Starts a classic Snake minigame.
* Snake speed and length increase with each target.
* Shows current score and high score.
* High score is saved to the save file.
